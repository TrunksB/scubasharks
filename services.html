Services

Diving and underwater Services are the core activities of the company. We are fully equipped to provide a comprehensive range of diving services to undertake any kind of underwater work. Our teams are led by highly talented and industry experienced people and supported by very competent personnel, trained to meet international standards and certified by both CONFEDERATION MONDIALE DES ACTIVITES SUBAQUATIQUES (CMAS) and Freediving Coaches of Asia (FCOA). Some of diving and underwater services provided by us, but not included all, are as mentioned below: -


Diving and Swimming Trainings with respective certifications


We train swimming, open water and advanced open water divers

Underwater installations such as foot-valves and cables


Let install those equipment for you

Underwater inspections, surveys and impact assessments


We help you determine the condition or damage of equipment underwater

Anchoring and Mooring services


We help secure infrastructure on water by deploying, inspecting and maintaing anchorage systems

Light salvage operations


Dont loose your equipment to the sea, we salvage them for you

Search and recovery operations


We help to search and recover lost goods, and precious items in shallow waters

Open water safety awareness


We train swimming and survival skills in open waters, educate communities on how to use their environment sustainably

General underwater maintenance work


We also do general maintenance works like hull cleaning


We have set up a dedicated staff to help personel interested in diving courses and fresher to complete their top up courses, and reassessments. We also take on the onus to provide them with jobs on many of our ongoing projects in Uganda after successful completion of their courses.
