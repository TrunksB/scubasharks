About Us

Scuba Sharks Uganda is a specialist underwater services provider to its offshore (Oil & Gas), onshore, Civil, Marine and Shipping Clients. Ideology behind the formation of this company was to provide all sorts of quality, cost effective and standard services to the owners as an extended arm of the owner, rather than a mere service provider. Therefore, we have assembled a team of highly expertise and skilled professionals in their respective fields, state of arts equipment and world class certification to provide diving services in various aspects; underwater engineering, project management, marine support, consultancies etc. all under one roof.
Scuba Sharks Uganda offers professional, prompt, efficient and competitive services. Our main strengths are loyalty, flexibility and ability to deliver the projects safely, on time and to the best possible quality. We strongly believe in business ethics, transparency and safety culture. Scuba Sharks Uganda provides diving services in East Africa up to full satisfaction of our clients.

OUR VISION

Our aim is to be one of the leading, integrated service providers over the globe. We will harness our knowledge and energy to provide world class quality by maintaining safety and environmental standards. We are deeply committed to excellence and reliable services by challenging the toughest issues and providing innovative solutions, while meeting projects on time and budget constraints with customer satisfaction.

    

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to be a premier company with total solutions, by providing diving, marine and offshore services to our customers through the integration of motivated people, quality equipment and to innovate with particular focus on a technically demanding environment. We will constantly upgrade our procedures, skills, systems and technology to create greater value for our clients, suppliers, and employees.
